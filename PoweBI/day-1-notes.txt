Business intelligence
Business intelligence is the process of collecting ,transforming ,analyzing,and visualizing data to help organizations make better decisions.

power bi converts data into 
*charts
*Dashboards
*KPIs
*Reports(MIS Reports)

What is power BI?
Power bi is Microsoft's business tool used to:
connect data 
clean and transform data
